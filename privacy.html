Kneel

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Kneel is a Christian prayer app designed to help users build a daily rhythm of prayer, reflection, and devotional consistency.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using Kneel.

By using Kneel, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Kneel is designed to collect only the information needed to provide the app experience, improve reliability, understand general product usage, and manage subscriptions.

Information You Provide

  • Your first name, if you choose to enter it during onboarding.
  • Your onboarding answers, such as prayer goals, preferred prayer time, life stage, and faith-related preferences.
  • Your notification preference and reminder time, if you choose to enable reminders.

Prayer and Progress Information

  • Whether you completed a daily prayer.
  • Your completed prayer dates.
  • Your unlocked stained glass collection progress.
  • Your prayer history shown inside the app.

Usage and Interaction Information

  • App screens opened, such as Home, History, Collection, Settings, onboarding, and paywall screens.
  • Basic interaction events, such as tapping buttons, opening plans, restoring purchases, or viewing collection items.
  • Paywall interaction events, including which paywall screens were viewed and how long certain paywall screens were viewed.

Account and Subscription Information

  • An anonymous user identifier created through Firebase Anonymous Authentication.
  • Subscription status, purchase attempts, restore attempts, and entitlement status through RevenueCat.
  • Store product information needed to display subscription options and process purchases.
Some onboarding answers may relate to your faith journey or prayer habits. We use this information only to personalize and improve the app experience. You should avoid entering information that you do not want associated with your anonymous app profile.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide daily prayer content and devotional progress features.
  • Show your prayer history and stained glass collection progress.
  • Personalize parts of the onboarding and app experience.
  • Send reminders if you enable notifications.
  • Process, validate, and restore subscriptions.
  • Understand which screens and features are being used.
  • Identify bugs, improve usability, and make product decisions.
  • Protect the app from misuse and maintain app reliability.

4. Third-Party Services

Kneel uses trusted third-party services to operate essential app functionality.

Firebase

We use Firebase Authentication and Firebase Firestore to create an anonymous user profile, store app progress, save onboarding-related answers, and record lightweight app usage events.

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions, determine whether you have premium access, process purchase-related status, and restore purchases.

Apple App Store

Purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple’s App Store payment system. Kneel does not receive or store your full payment card details.

5. Subscriptions and Purchases

If you purchase a subscription, the transaction is handled by Apple through your App Store account. RevenueCat helps us verify whether your subscription is active so the app can unlock the appropriate features.

We may store purchase-related status, such as whether a purchase was attempted, cancelled, failed, restored, or successfully activated. This helps us debug subscription issues and improve the purchase experience.

6. Notifications

Kneel may ask for permission to send daily prayer reminders. Notifications are optional. You can enable, disable, or change notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

If you choose a reminder time, that preference may be stored locally on your device and may also be used to schedule your daily reminder.

7. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect the information processed through Kneel. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Some data is stored locally on your device using Apple-provided storage mechanisms. Some app usage, onboarding, and subscription-related data may be stored using Firebase and RevenueCat.

8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, maintain your progress, support subscriptions, improve the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve technical issues.

If you reset your progress inside the app, some locally stored prayer progress may be removed from your device. Server-side analytics, subscription records, or technical logs may remain where needed for reliability, fraud prevention, legal, or business purposes.

9. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain personal information.

You can contact us to request deletion or review of data associated with your app usage. Because Kneel uses anonymous authentication, we may need information from your app instance or device context to locate the relevant anonymous account.

You can also:

  • Disable notifications in your device settings.
  • Cancel or manage subscriptions through your Apple App Store account.
  • Use the reset progress option inside the app to clear certain local progress data.
  • Stop using the app and delete it from your device.

10. Children’s Privacy

Kneel is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

11. International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. By using Kneel, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in locations where our service providers operate.

12. We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your prayer data or onboarding answers for third-party advertising.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of Kneel after changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
