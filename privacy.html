Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Kneel handles information when you use our daily prayer, reflection, reminder, history, stained glass collection, and subscription features.

Last updated: May 14, 2026 Owner: Enes Keskin Contact: keskkeness@hotmail.com

1. Overview

Kneel is a Christian daily prayer app designed to help users read a daily prayer, maintain a devotional habit, receive optional reminders, view prayer history, and unlock stained glass collection pieces through completed prayer days.

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible. Most prayer progress and app activity data is stored locally on your device and is used only to provide the app experience.

2. Information We Collect

Prayer progress and app activity

Kneel may store app progress on your device, including completed prayer days, unlocked stained glass pieces, prayer history entries, and the prayer key associated with a completed day.

  • This information is primarily stored locally on your device.
  • It is used to display your prayer history, collection progress, and completed days.
  • Kneel does not require you to create an account to use these local progress features.

Notification preferences

If you enable daily reminders, Kneel may store your reminder preference and selected reminder time on your device. Notifications are scheduled only after you grant permission through iOS.

Purchases and subscriptions

If you purchase a subscription, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card number, banking details, or Apple ID password.

Kneel may use Apple’s in-app purchase system and third-party subscription infrastructure such as RevenueCat to validate subscription status, restore purchases, and manage access to premium features.

Support and feedback

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and the information you choose to include in your message. We use this information only to respond to your request, provide support, troubleshoot issues, and improve the app.

Technical information

Apple, the App Store, and subscription-related services may process limited technical information necessary to provide the app, process purchases, prevent fraud, and maintain app functionality.

3. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

Kneel does not intentionally require or collect:

  • Your real-world identity beyond what you voluntarily provide in support emails
  • Your phone number
  • Your precise location
  • Your contacts
  • Your photos or camera access
  • Your microphone access
  • Your health data
  • Your private religious affiliation beyond your voluntary use of a Christian prayer app

4. How We Use Information

We use information only for app-related purposes, including to:

  • Display daily prayers and reflections
  • Track completed prayer days
  • Show prayer history and collection progress
  • Schedule local daily reminders when enabled
  • Manage subscription access and restore purchases
  • Respond to support and feedback requests
  • Maintain and improve the app experience

5. Local Storage

Kneel stores certain progress and settings locally on your device. This may include completed prayer days, reminder time, notification preference, prayer history markers, and collection progress.

If you delete the app, reset progress, or clear app data, locally stored progress may be removed and may not be recoverable.

6. Notifications

Daily reminders are optional. You may enable or disable notifications in the app settings or through your device settings.

Kneel uses reminders to help you return to your prayer habit. We do not use notifications to collect sensitive personal information.

7. Subscriptions and App Store Purchases

Paid features may be offered through auto-renewable subscriptions or other in-app purchases. Purchases are handled by Apple through your App Store account.

  • Apple processes your payment information.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings.
  • We may use subscription validation services to determine whether premium access is active.

8. Third-Party Services

Kneel may rely on third-party services for limited app functionality, including:

  • Apple App Store and StoreKit for purchases and subscription management
  • Apple Push Notification services for reminders, if enabled
  • RevenueCat for subscription validation and entitlement management, if used in the app

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with advertisers.

We may share limited information only when necessary to operate the app, process purchases, respond to support requests, comply with legal obligations, or protect the security and integrity of the app.

10. Children’s Privacy

Kneel is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us at keskkeness@hotmail.com and we will take appropriate steps.

11. Data Retention

Local app progress remains on your device until you delete the app, reset progress, or otherwise remove the data. Support emails may be retained as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request and maintain support records.

12. Your Choices

  • You can disable notifications in the app or device settings.
  • You can reset progress inside the app settings.
  • You can manage subscriptions through your Apple ID settings.
  • You can contact us to ask questions about this policy.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
