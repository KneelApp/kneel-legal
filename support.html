Common Help Topics

Restoring purchases

If you purchased a subscription but premium access is not active, open Kneel, go to Settings, and tap “Restore Purchases.”

Managing or canceling subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Open iOS Settings, tap your Apple ID, then Subscriptions. You can also use the “Manage in App Store” button inside Kneel Settings.

Daily reminders

If reminders are not arriving, check that notifications are allowed for Kneel in iOS Settings. Also confirm that the reminder is enabled inside Kneel Settings.

Prayer history

Prayer history is based on completed prayer days stored by the app. If progress is reset or app data is deleted, previous local history may no longer be available.

Collection progress

Stained glass collection pieces unlock through completed prayer days. If pieces do not appear as expected, contact support and describe the day count shown in the app.

When Contacting Support

To help us respond faster, include:

  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • The Kneel app version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Whether the issue is related to subscriptions, reminders, history, collection, or app content

Response Time

We aim to respond to support emails within 2–3 business days. Response times may vary depending on volume, weekends, holidays, and the complexity of the request.
